Q & A

  • What's Avocado?
    It’s not built to replace fancy task managers out there; rather it’s an attempt to enhance them by giving you a dead simple interface to interact with your tasks and actually do them - not to fiddle with them.
  • How it works?
    You use your normal task manager to decide what you want to do today. Those tasks will show up in Avocado. That’s it.
  • Can I pause a pomodoro?
    You can’t because your attention doesn’t work this way - you either focus full time or start over.
  • What if I need to go to the bathroom?
    You have our permission to count that as work, keep the pomodoro going.
