Productivity enhancer

What?

productivity enhancer, not task manager

Whaaat?

It’s not built to replace fancy task managers out there; rather it’s an attempt to enhance them by giving you a dead simple interface to interact with your tasks and actually do them - not to fiddle with them.

  • dead simple interface focused on doing - not managing your tasks
  • mouseless interaction
  • daily pomodoro goal
  • delightful sounds
  • todoist, gtasks, (insert your tool) fetch
